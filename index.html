• About the Game

A Rhythmic Journey Through Geometry

A Dance of Fire and Ice is a deceptively simple, precision rhythm game. You control two orbiting planets with just one button — tapping to the beat. Each level introduces unique paths and shapes that teach you to "hear" geometry. It's not about visual cues — it's about feeling the rhythm.

• How to Play

How to Play

Step 1
Tap in time with the beat to move forward
Step 2
Every shape has its rhythm — learn how circles, triangles, and squares sound
Step 3
Don’t rush — if you tap off-beat, your planets break orbit
Step 4
Some tracks are slow and dreamy, others blisteringly fast. Adapt!
Step 5
Use sound, not sight. Close your eyes if you must. Trust the rhythm.

• Difficulty

Hard. But Fair

This is not your typical rhythm game. You won’t be spammed with notes — instead, you’ll be asked to maintain perfect timing for every beat. It will challenge your focus, patience, and listening skills. But it will never be random.

You will fail. You will restart. You will improve.

FAQ

  • Is A Dance of Fire and Ice free to play?

    Yes! The base game is free with optional level expansions and updates.

  • Do I need an internet connection?

    Nope. You can play offline.

  • Is it beginner friendly?

    It starts simple, but ramps up. If you're new to rhythm games, expect a challenge!

  • Are new levels added?

    Yes! The game receives updates with new themed levels and songs.

  • How do I calibrate?

    Use the calibration tool in settings. Follow instructions carefully and use headphones.

  • What devices are supported?

    Android 6.0+ recommended. iOS and desktop support available via Steam.
